About Doctor

Dr. Omar Faruqi Dr. Omar Faruqi is a highly dedicated and experienced pediatrician who is committed to providing exceptional healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. With a strong background in clinical medicine and years of hands-on experience, he has developed expertise in diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide range of childhood illnesses. His approach focuses not only on curing diseases but also on preventive care, ensuring that every child grows in a healthy and safe environment. Known for his compassionate and patient-friendly nature, Dr. Omar takes the time to understand each child’s condition and explains treatments clearly to parents, building trust and confidence. His clinic is equipped with modern facilities, and he continuously updates his knowledge to provide the best and most advanced medical care possible. Parents appreciate his calm demeanor, attention to detail, and commitment to the overall well-being of every child he treats.
